Workshop Schedule

Day 1: Fundamentals

10:00 AM – 11:45 AM Intro to Robotics & Basic Electronics
12:00 PM – 01:10 PM Mechanical Fundamentals & Motion
01:40 PM – 04:00 PM Hands‑On Non‑Programmable Projects (Street light, laser security, etc.)

Day 2: Programmable Robotics

10:00 AM – 11:00 AM Actuators, Motors & Selection
11:15 AM – 12:40 PM Batteries & Power Management
01:20 PM – 04:30 PM Programmable Robotics Projects (Line follower, obstacle avoidance, etc.)

Expected Learning Outcomes

Understand the basic structure and components of robotic systems.
Build and test simple electronic circuits used in robotics.
Understand motor selection, battery systems, and power management.
Develop basic programming and interfacing skills using microcontrollers.
Design and test simple mobile robots.
