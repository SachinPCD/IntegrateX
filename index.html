About Gen AI Spark

Gen AI Spark Workshop

IETE-ISF VBIT • Department of ECE

The IETE-ISF VBIT, in association with the Department of ECE, proudly presents Gen AI Spark — a comprehensive one-day immersive workshop on Generative AI technologies.

Workshop Speaker

Mrs. Santhi Swarupa Chebrole Founder & CEO, Delightech Solutions, Hyderabad

This session provides participants with a structured learning environment to explore cutting-edge Generative Artificial Intelligence. Through hands-on exercises, attendees will develop practical skills in AI prototyping and implementation.

Learning Outcomes

Hands-on experience with ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot
Ethical AI design principles and best practices
Creative problem-solving methodologies
Prototype validation and testing techniques

This workshop aims to foster innovation, enhance technical understanding, and inspire engineering students to integrate creative thinking with technological expertise — transforming conceptual ideas into practical, intelligent solutions.
